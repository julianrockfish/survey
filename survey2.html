TThis survey is a demonstration is a showcase of the functionality of Usabilla. Your survey will appear immediately on the right hand side of the screen.

The core focus of this product is visual and accurate user feedback for site improvement. Usabilla’s JavaScript fancybox, allows user to select the item on the page they would like to give feedback on, and this is captured along with their answers to provided questions.

Usabilla

Usabilla was founded in Amsterdam by a team of user experience researchers in 2009, and hosts 2B feedback buttons per month with a customer base including Vodafone, Disney, Levi’s, EA, booking.com, Hewlett-Packard, T-Mobile, News Corp and more.

Targeting

Usabilla campaigns allow targeting users based on path, time on page, returning or new, device, number of pages viewed, location on page, and potential to exit site (for exit surveys).
