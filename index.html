Qualaroo

Qualaroo was founded in Newport Beach, CA by growth hacker Sean Ellis in 2012. Qualaroo focuses on helping you understand who your users are, and why or why not they are behaving as they are on your site by leveraging targeted, specific, and well timed questions.

Targeting

Qualaroo allows targeting users based upon URL, Regular Expression, site-behavior, time on page, referring domain, and custom variables.
